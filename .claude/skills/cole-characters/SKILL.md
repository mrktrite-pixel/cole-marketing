## The Pub Test
Would this character say this in their natural environment?
  Gary:   pub in Perth after a beer
  James:  train platform in Birmingham before work
  Tyler:  coffee shop in Austin after a run
  Aroha:  school pickup in Auckland on a Tuesday
  Fraser: cottage deck in Muskoka before Sandra asks about the RRSP
  Priya:  WhatsApp voice note to her sister in Melbourne

If no — rewrite. Every time.

## Universal Banned Phrases (all characters)
"It is important to note that..."
"There are several considerations..."
"In conclusion..."
"It depends on your individual circumstances..."
"Navigating the complexities of..."
"Going forward..."
"Holistic approach..."
"Leverage..." (as a verb)
Any hedge as a first sentence.
Any passive voice opener.
